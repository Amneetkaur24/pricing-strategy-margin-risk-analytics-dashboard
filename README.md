# Pricing Margin Risk Analytics


## Objective
To enable leadership to evaluate discount strategies while proactively managing margin risk.

## Business Question
Which discount levels maximize revenue without increasing exposure to margin risk?
## What This Dashboard Delivers
- Revenue, profit, margin, and order KPIs
- Margin risk segmentation (Healthy / Medium / High)
- Discount band impact on profitability
- Category-level revenue and risk analysis
- Order-level drilldown for high-risk cases
- Interactive filtering by region, category, and business unit


## Tools & Technologies

• Microsoft Excel  
• PivotTables & PivotCharts  
• Data modeling using structured tables  
• Interactive filtering with slicers  
• VBA macros for dashboard navigation  
• Business KPI dashboard design

## Key Insight

• Medium discount levels generate the best balance between revenue growth and margin stability.

• High discounts increase revenue but significantly increase the proportion of high-risk orders.

• Certain product categories contribute disproportionately to margin risk during aggressive discount campaigns.

## Business Impact

This dashboard enables leadership teams to evaluate pricing strategies while controlling margin risk.

By identifying discount levels that drive revenue without significantly increasing high-risk orders, the analysis helps support data-driven pricing decisions and protects overall profitability.
